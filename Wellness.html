Wellness & Health

As a college student, it is important to maintain a healthy and balanced lifestyle. Below, I will be highlighting a few businesses that promote wellness in multiple functions.

Working out is an essential activity and you can do so at Vbody Power Fitness. The gym is located is in the Bronzeville area at 900 E 47 st., also know as at the corner of 47th & Drexel. They pride themselves on producing more results in weight loss, body fat loss, and strength gain than any gym in the Chicago Area. Be sure to check them out!

Another black owned gym is “The Well Chicago”. Cycle therapy was founded by Aaron Foster and his wife Aniya Foster. Their combined love for fitness inspired them to create “The Well Chi'' and they brand themselves as more than a gym but as a place that is powered by the people it serves to fuel bodies, feed spirits, and fill souls. Like the reciprocal power of water, The Well restores balance facilitating the flow of healing and wellness into oneself and others. We accomplish this through high-energy, body-sculpting fitness classes, mental health awareness, and community-building initiatives.
